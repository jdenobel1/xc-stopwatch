Stopwatch Instructions

Here are the instructions for using the stopwatch:

  • Click the "Start" button to begin the timer.
  • Click the "Stop" button to pause the timer.
  • Bib Entry textbox --> enter the bib numbers if athletes are wearing as they finish and press spacebar to log
  • Click the "Reset" button to reset the lap/place counter and entered bibs, and timer to 00:00:00.00.
  • Click the "Record Lap" button to record lap times. Records and displays the Laps/Place and Time. Only use either the button itself or can press spacebar but only use one through the use of the stopwatch
  • Click the "Export Laps" button to save lap times to a file. You will prompted to name the file
